QUESTIONS

The questions everyone asks before saying yes.

How much does a website cost? +
Our packages start at ₹9,999 for a basic business website and go up depending on features, pages, and complexity. Before any work begins, you'll receive a detailed quote — no hidden costs, ever.
How long does it take to build a website? +
Most standard websites are delivered in 7–14 working days. E-commerce stores and complex projects may take up to 21 days. We'll give you an exact timeline in your proposal.
I'm not tech-savvy. Will I understand everything? +
Absolutely yes. We avoid technical jargon and explain everything in plain language. You'll always know exactly what's happening with your project.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
Just your business information, any photos or content you already have, and a rough idea of what you want. We guide you through everything else.
Can I update my website myself after it's done? +
Yes! We build websites with easy-to-use CMS systems so you can update text, images, and products without touching any code. We also provide a quick tutorial session.
What happens after the website is launched? +
Every project includes 30 days of free support after launch. After that, we offer affordable maintenance packages. And honestly? We're just a WhatsApp message away — always.
